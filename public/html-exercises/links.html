Top Picks: A blog of my Bests and Greatests

My Top 3 Favorite movies

The third spot goes to Percy Jackson & the Olympians:The Lightning Thief . This is one of my favorites because I am fascinated even as a child about greek and Roman Mythology. I often read my sister's books in school about mythology. This movie is based from the book series written by Rick Riordan. It is a good story about how the ancient greek myths aren't just myths and they still exist to this day.

Next is Troy. I love this movie because aside from it depicts one of the most epic wars in Greek mythology is that it has a lot of action. it's effects are also decent considering that it was first released in 2004. I read about the Iliad where the trojan war was illustrated when I was in grade 4 that is why I really wanted to watch this movie then and a lot more times to this day.

Finally, the top spot goes to The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King. This is my ultimate favorite movie because it has a lot of war scenes and it is the conclusion to the epic The Lord of the Rings trilogy. It has also a decent visual effects then when it aired in 2003 but when I watched it again, the effects doesn't seem very good but the fight scenes are still very exhilarating so I watch it still every once in a while.
